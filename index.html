About

GradConnect was inspired by my own journey after graduation. Struggling to find the right opportunities and navigate the job market, I realized the importance of having a mentor to guide me through the process. This project started [timeline] as a way to bridge the gap between graduates and industry professionals. It has been an incredible learning experience and is now a Portfolio Project for Holberton School. You can find more details about Holberton School here.
